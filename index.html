About Me

Making the complex feel obvious.

I'm Peace Sandy, a technical writer with a passion for making complex technical concepts accessible. I write for developers by truly understanding what they build.

My work spans API reference documentation, developer tutorials, technical articles, and product docs. I've contributed to open source projects including Cilium documentation, and I lead a community of aspiring technical writers through mentorship, content reviews, and writing challenges.

When I'm not writing docs, I'm speaking at tech events, contributing to open source, and helping new technical writers find their footing.
